Work

Facebook's Atlas is a digital advertising platform for brands and ad agencies. I helped their design team craft and prototype the UX prior to launch.

GeoEngineers is an earth science and technology firm with 14 offices. I did user research, UX design and prototyping of a new company intranet.

Microsoft Windows, SharePoint, and Advertising groups have been my clients on a variety of website, intranet, and extranet UX projects.

Motif Investing is an online broker that lets investors buy stocks based on ideas and trends. I helped them design and prototype a new iPhone app.

National Geographic needed a set of interactives to educate web visitors on global warming. I helped research, organize and present the content.

Blink is a UX research and design firm specializing in digital products. I led numerous interaction design and research projects for the company.

Fell Swoop is a digital agency with a focus on user experience. I was a partner and lead designer on a wide range of design and research projects.

frog is one of the best product design firms in the world. I embedded with their team to help design and prototype a cloud-based collaboration app.

Expertise

User Experience Design

User profiles, personas, task analysis, scenarios, user flows, user stories, experience mapping.

Interaction Design

UI design, paper prototyping, wireframe prototyping, interactive prototyping, high fidelity wireframes.

Information Architecture

Navigation design, labeling, content inventory, taxonomy, metadata, tagging, search design.

User Research

Contextual inquiry, user interviews, user surveys, card sort studies, mental models.

Usability Testing

Test plan, participant screener, session script, remote testing, in-person moderation, findings analysis, recommendations report.

Expert Review

Heuristic analysis, cognitive walkthrough, issue identification, severity ranking, recommendations report.

My process is based on established user-centered design methods, but is adjusted and optimized for each situation and client.
